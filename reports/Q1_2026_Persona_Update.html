Executive Summary

This quarterly update analyzes 100,183 real Gong calls from SMB (50-249 employees) and Commercial (250-1,499 employees) segments over 6 months.

Key Changes:

  • AI language shift — 72-73% of calls mention AI/automation. Buyers say "automation" not "AI" (especially SMB)
  • Procurement earlier — Finance/Procurement appearing at demo stage in Commercial
  • Agent-assist over chatbots — Focus shifted from customer-facing AI to agent productivity
  • WFM/QA emerging — Operations Leader persona increasingly involved (45% of Commercial deals)
  • Implementation speed — "Time to value" now #1 evaluation criteria for SMB

📈 Key Insights from Real Data

AI/Automation is EVERYWHERE

72-73% of all calls mention AI, automation, or intelligent features. This is significantly higher than expected and validates the AI language shift identified in secondary research.

Pain Points Consistently Discussed

Commercial Buyers More Concerned

Commercial segment shows 27% pain point discussion rate (vs 23% for SMB), suggesting more complex challenges and higher scrutiny in mid-market deals.
